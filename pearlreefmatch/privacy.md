# Pearl Reef Match — Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** April 30, 2026

**Effective Date:** April 30, 2026

---

Dear User,

Thank you for choosing **Pearl Reef Match**. We highly value your privacy and the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how Pearl Reef Match handles your information when you use this application.

By accessing or using the application, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**Pearl Reef Match does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.**

The application functions entirely offline and requires no account or login. We do not collect:

- Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
- Device identifiers (IDFA, advertising ID)
- Location data
- Contacts, photos, microphone, or camera data
- Browsing or usage analytics
- Any data from third-party services

---

## 2. Local Storage

All game progress (scores, unlocked levels, settings, achievements) is stored only on your device using iOS standard local storage (UserDefaults / PlayerPrefs).

This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not transmitted to any server
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Will be deleted automatically when you uninstall the app

You may reset all local progress at any time through the in-app **Settings → Reset Progress** option.

---

## 3. Third-Party Services

Pearl Reef Match does not integrate with any third-party services. There are no:

- Advertising networks
- Analytics SDKs
- Social media plugins
- Cloud sync services
- External APIs or networking calls

The app works completely offline and requires no internet connection.

---

## 4. Children's Privacy

Pearl Reef Match is rated 4+ and is designed to be safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13, and the app's offline-only design ensures full compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

---

## 5. Data Security

Because Pearl Reef Match does not collect or transmit personal information, there is no risk of remote data breach. All game data is protected by your device's standard iOS security measures.

---

## 6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal information, there is no personal data for us to access, correct, or delete on our servers. You retain full control over local game data on your device, and may delete it at any time by uninstalling the app or using the in-app reset option.

---

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in legal requirements or app functionality. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will indicate when the policy was last revised. Continued use of the app after such changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
